    • About

    TEDx IITRoorkee is organized by a group of enthusiasts who form a part of the Entrepreneurship Cell IIT Roorkee. The spirit of TED aligns closely with the primary aspiration of the E-CELL, which is to foster the dissemination of knowledge, innovation, and inspiration. Since its inception in 2010, TEDx IITRoorkee has now become an annual event, playing host to a multitude of speakers from various walks of life. TEDxIITRoorkee boasts of an active following online, through its website and on various social media channels. This year, it is coming back on March 23rd, 2024.

    Frequently asked questions

    What is TEDx 24-25?

    TEDx 24-25 is a locally organized event that brings together thought leaders to share ideas worth spreading.

    Where is the event held?

    The event will be held at IIT Roorkee, with online streaming available.

    How can I attend?

    Tickets will be available on our website soon. Stay tuned!

    Can I suggest a speaker for TEDx 24-25?

    Absolutely! We are always open to hearing about amazing individuals who have ideas worth spreading. Please submit your speaker suggestion through the form on our website.

    How do I become a sponsor for TEDx 24-25?

    If you’re interested in sponsoring TEDx 24-25, please reach out to our sponsorship team through the contact form on our website. We offer various sponsorship packages that can be tailored to your needs.

    Can I volunteer at TEDx 24-25?

    Yes! We welcome volunteers to help make the event a success. If you’re interested in volunteering, please fill out the volunteer application form on our website.
